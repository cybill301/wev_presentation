ST. LAWRENCE MENUS

Staying by popular demand, we’re pleased to offer our continued series of monthly Table d’Hôte menus inspired by celebrated culinary regions and influences. Tickets for our monthly three-course menu include a choice of starter, main and dessert, with optional add-ons for the table.

BRASSERIE PARISIENNE

Tuesdays through Sundays – March - April 2022
Brasseries are a Parisian institution and can be traced back to the 1870’s, defining the ‘City of Light.’ For the months of March and April, we are pleased to bring the the tradition to Vancouver with our Brasserie Parisienne menu.

GASCONY-BASQUE

Tuesdays through Sundays – May 3 to June 19, 2022
Once called the “most delicious corner of France” by the New York Times, the Gascony-Basque region may not be as familiar as some of the country’s culinary destinations, but its undeniably rich farmhouse cooking makes instant fans of all who try it. Find out why with our Gascony-Basque Country menu this May and June.

OUR TEAM

J-C POIRIER

OWNER/EXECUTIVE CHEF

Originally from Quebec, J-C has been a fixture on the Canadian culinary scene for more than 25 years. After voyaging west in 2004 and helming kitchens at a number of notable Vancouver restaurants he launched passion project St. Lawrence in 2017, where he indulges his razor-sharp focus, dedication to craft, and affinity for time-honoured traditions.

LANDEN SWICK

CHEF DE CUSINE

Landen believes that competition and collaboration make the ideal ingredients for a winning kitchen team. As an ambitious French-trained chef, Landen has a deep respect for classic techniques and is constantly striving to finesse his culinary skills under the guidance of J-C Poirier.

JULIE SOPUCK

GENERAL MANAGER

The dinner table is a magical place and Julie facilitates those special moments with style, precision and confidence. As a natural leader with experience in Vancouver’s top dining rooms, she demonstrates an acute attention to detail while presiding over the room each night to deliver a flawless guest experience.

SARAH HAWKINS

BAR MANAGER

Sarah earned her stripes behind the pine at a number of well-known establishments in Toronto, Alberta and Vancouver before joining the St. Lawrence family in 2018. She prides herself on curating a bar program comprising equal parts spirit-forward, time-tested classics and generous dashes of conviviality and creativity.

DAVID LAWSON

SOMMELIER & DIRECTEUR ADJOINT

David began his hospitality career in Canada’s Capital, though the seasoned hospitality veteran has worked at restaurants across the continent. No matter where he travelled, he missed the culture of his home region along the Ontario-Quebec border. David’s passion for lively and energetic wines made by skilled low-intervention winemakers are showcased throughout the wine list and he’s excited to share his expertise, and French-Canadian roots with diners.
